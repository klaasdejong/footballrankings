How the Impact slider works: each match is decided by a blend of luck and FIFA ranking points. At 0% every match is a pure coin flip — rankings are completely ignored. At 100% the stronger team wins every single match without exception. In between, the size of the points gap between the two teams matters: France vs Haiti (≈700 pt gap) will be heavily one-sided at high impact, while two closely ranked sides stay near 50/50 no matter the setting. Think of it as a dial from "anything can happen on the day" to "quality always tells".
